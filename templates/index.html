Submit Model Year

Please enter a model year (e.g., 2020) to retrieve information about electric vehicles from Washington's Department of Licensing.

The data will include:
  • The total number of electric vehicles for that model year
  • The average electric range (in miles) of vehicles for that year
  • Data grouped by the make of the vehicle (e.g., Tesla, Nissan)
Once you submit, the results will be displayed on the next page.
